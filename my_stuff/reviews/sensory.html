Sensory: Life on the Spectrum" is a vibrant and heartfelt anthology that offers a unique glimpse into the lives of autistic individuals through the medium of comics. this collection features contributions from thirty autistic creators, each sharing their personal experiences, challenges, and triumphs. The comics cover a wide range of topics, from the journey to diagnosis to finding community and coping mechanisms for sensory overload. The illustrations are as diverse as the stories themselves, ranging from soft, paint-like depictions to jagged, dark drawings, each adding a distinct emotional depth to the narratives. Overall, "Sensory: Life on the Spectrum" is a must-read for anyone looking to explore the autistic experience through a creative and engaging format. It's a testament to the strength and resilience of autistic individuals and a celebration of their diverse voices.
